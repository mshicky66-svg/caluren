How Caluren works

Your displayCaluren stays on and shows a live counter, your photos, and a countdown to your next big day.
Settings — gear icon, top rightTap the gear to open settings and change names, date, photos, and timing.
Couple, Family, or BothCouple shows your relationship counter. Family rotates through your kids. Both shows both.
PhotosAdd up to 6 photos. They rotate automatically. Tap a photo to crop or replace it.
Settings are savedEverything you set is saved to this device and remembered after restarts.
