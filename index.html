How Caluren works

Your displayCaluren stays on and shows a live counter, your photos, and a countdown to your next big day. Just plug it in, connect WiFi, and add your photos.
Settings — the gear, top rightTap the gear to open settings and customize everything below.
Couple, Family, or BothCouple shows your names and how long you've been together. Family rotates through your kids, showing each one's age and birthday countdown. Both shows the couple screen and the kids, switching between them.
Names and dateSet both names and your "together since" date. The counter and anniversary countdown update on their own.
ChildrenIn Family or Both, add each child with a name, birthday, and photo. The display shows their age and counts down to their birthday.
PhotosAdd as many photos as you want and tap one to crop it. They roll automatically in the slideshow.
TimingChoose how fast photos change. In Both mode you also set how long the couple stays before switching to a child.
Share yoursTap "Share yours" to send your setup to someone. They'll open it and see your display exactly as you made it — names, dates, kids, and photos.
